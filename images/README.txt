INSTRUCTIONS FOR ADDING YOUR PHOTO:
====================================

1. Place your professional photo in this folder
2. Name it: profile-photo.jpg
   (or profile-photo.png if it's a PNG file)

3. Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png

4. Recommended size: 
   - Minimum: 500x500 pixels
   - Optimal: 1000x1000 pixels or larger
   - Square format works best

5. After adding your photo, refresh your browser to see it!

NOTE: If your photo has a different name or format, you'll need to update the image source in index.html:
   - Look for: src="images/profile-photo.jpg"
   - Change to match your file name
